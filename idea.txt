Whack-a-mole
For a particular digit of their verification code, user will need to play a whack-a-mole game of different digits (as the moles)
and the user would need to specifically whack (with their cursor) their digit a specific amount of times within the time limit.
If they miss one, they will need to redo it. If they hit the wrong number, they will lose 2s on their timer.
If they whack it correctly then their counter for the correctly-hit whacks will increase by one.

Coding:
Use Python, Pygame, etc...
Will need to use classes, inheritance, attributes, 2D lists, sorting/searching, algorithms, encapsulation (maybe), class & static methods, operator overloading (maybe), recursion (maybe), File I/O (maybe)

UI wise:
At the top have the "Enter Your Verification Code" with the 6-digit box thing below, and then the submit button below that.
For the rest of the screen below, we will have a bunch of holes, and the user's cursor will be a hammer (need an image for the hammer). 
The "moles" are simply just the numbers themselves (so need an image for each number).
In the bottom left corner of the screen, we will have "Digit: {user's digit}" and then right below that, it will have the "Counter: {how many times the user has correctly whacked this digit}"
In the bottom right corner of the screen, have a "Timer:" text and then a timer thing below that like "00:00s".
In the bottom middle, have in small font "Whack your digit specififcally your digit number of times within the time limit!" or something more clearer.
